FAQ
Questions we always get.
Is this real legal advice? +
No — Signova is an AI-powered analysis tool for informational purposes. It helps you understand your contracts and identify risks, but it's not a substitute for a qualified lawyer. For high-value or complex agreements, we always recommend consulting a legal professional.
What file types does Signova support? +
Signova supports PDF (text and scanned), JPG, PNG, and WEBP. For scanned documents, we use Google Vision OCR to extract text before analysis — so even blurry phone photos of contracts work reasonably well.
Which countries and legal systems does it cover? +
Signova covers 40+ countries including Malaysia, Singapore, USA, UK, Australia, Canada, and most of Southeast Asia and Europe. During onboarding, you set your jurisdiction and Signova tailors every analysis to your local laws.
Is my data secure? +
Yes. Your contracts are encrypted at rest and in transit. We never share your documents with third parties. You can delete your data at any time from your account settings.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Absolutely. No long-term commitments. Cancel your subscription any time from your dashboard and you won't be charged again. Your contracts remain accessible until the end of your billing period.
